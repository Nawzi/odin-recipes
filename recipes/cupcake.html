Three Bean Salad With Celery

Description

Super yummy, light lemon taste. A different thing to try is to put the cake
mixture into large muffin tins to make a bunch of little cakes, you just need to
reduce the baking time. Let cake cool before frosting or glaze while still slightly warm.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder/li>
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup low-fat buttermilk
